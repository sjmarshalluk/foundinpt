Clara Wren

Fibre artist

About

Clara Wren has been weaving and dyeing natural fibres in Port Townsend for over a decade. Her work draws on the tidal rhythms and muted palette of the Pacific Northwest coast — seagrass, driftwood, winter light. She works primarily with wool, linen, and hand-harvested plant dyes, producing wall pieces, garments, and limited-run textiles sold through her studio and selected galleries.

Her studio is open by appointment most Saturdays, and she runs occasional natural dyeing workshops through the Port Townsend School of the Arts.
